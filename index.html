    Is Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu at 10th Planet suitable for females?

    Never. Anyone, at any age, can do Jiu-Jitsu. 10th Planet Perry has students ranging in age from 6 to 60.

    A typical class at 10th Planet Perry lasts about an hour and a half. Classes start with a short warm up and stretch. 30 to 40 minutes is spent working on techniques and then sparring or live drilling for 15 to 20 minutes.

    Comfortable clothing like shorts, t-shirt, tracksuit bottoms, etc. will be fine for your first day of training with us.

    Totally optional. The majority of people who learn and train Jiu Jitsu do not compete. Of course, competition can be a reason to set goals and a great way to challenge and test yourself.

    Absolutely! One of the main goals of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu is the use of technique and leverage to control, protect and even submit a much larger opponent. Therefore it is perfectly suited for females to use for self defense.
